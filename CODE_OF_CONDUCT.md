# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to keep Hatchery Core a respectful, technical, and productive project.
Participants are expected to communicate in good faith, be direct without being
hostile, and focus review on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of differing experience levels and constraints.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions on topic.
- Assume mistakes are fixable and ask for clarification when needed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Demeaning language or repeated bad-faith disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would make normal technical collaboration unsafe.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, discussions, and other
project spaces to enforce this code. Serious or repeated violations may result
in temporary or permanent loss of participation privileges.

Report concerns privately to security@axolotl.ai.
